Introduction

We are the providers of Edutainment a solution to all learners/ students in the easiest and interesting way and at their doorstep. We will be providing a solution for every little concept be it theory, hypothesis, theorem, Stem Education, Fascinating and sensitive topics relevant to society such as Global Warming, Poverty, Pollution, Illiteracy, Corruption, Inequality, Gender discrimination, Terrorism, Violence against Women, and many more at a very early age and in a sustainable manner on the 7D/9D platform. Interestingly the complete concept is based on wheels to provide a useful and convenient tool to the students of all age groups that can be run not only in tier1 cities but in rural and in interiors also. In this way, we can provide an easy solution to the students as an alternative to the books and will provide a real-time experience to them. The complete idea is based on the concept of providing Education on Wheels for its swift movement, hence the name “EduOnWheels”.
